{define: Cream frets 5 1 2 2 fingers 4 1 3 2}
{define: Pie frets 2 1 1 4 fingers 2 1 1 4 add: string 1 fret 1 finger 1 add: string 4 fret 1 finger 1}

# include fingers to be helpful!
